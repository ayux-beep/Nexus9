Your Privacy Matters

Last updated: May 2026 · Nexus v2.0

What We Collect

Account Information

When you sign up, we collect your name, username, and password. Your password is never stored in plain text — it is securely handled on your device.

Any optional info you add — bio, gender, website link — is only used to display on your profile.

Profile Photo

Your profile picture is stored locally on your own device. Nexus does not upload your photo to any external server. It stays with you, always.

Messages

All messages you send are stored locally on your device only. We do not read, scan, or share your conversations with anyone — ever.

What We Don't Do

❌ We do not sell your data to advertisers.

❌ We do not track your location.

❌ We do not share your information with third parties.

❌ We do not show ads based on your activity.

✅ Nexus is and always will be ad-free.

Your Rights

Delete Your Data

You can sign out and clear your browser's local storage at any time to permanently remove all your data from this device.

Data Portability

Your data belongs to you. Since everything is stored locally, you are always in full control of it.

Contact

For any privacy concerns or questions, reach out to us at:

privacy@nexus.app

What is Nexus?

Nexus is a modern, privacy-first messaging and social platform built for real human connection. No algorithms deciding what you see. No ads interrupting your flow. Just you and the people you care about.

We believe communication should be fast, beautiful, and completely private.

Features

💬 Real-time Messaging

Send and receive messages with near-zero delay. Clean, distraction-free chat interface.

🔍 People Search

Find and connect with anyone on Nexus by their username or real name. Follow, message, and build your network.

👤 Rich Profiles

Customize your profile with a photo, bio, gender, and website link. Show the world who you are.

🔔 Smart Notifications

Get notified when someone follows you. Follow back right from the notification — no extra steps.

🛡️ Admin Panel

Built-in moderation tools for platform admins. Monitor users, remove bad actors, and keep the community safe.

Our Values

🔒 Privacy first — Your data never leaves your device.

⚡ Speed — Responses in under a second, every time.

🎨 Design — Beautiful, modern UI that feels great to use.

🚫 No ads, ever — Nexus will always be ad-free.
